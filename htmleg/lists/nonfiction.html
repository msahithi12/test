Non-fiction books

It is literature that, regardless of the subject matter, has a simple goal:
to provide information.
It should be based on facts and conclusions of the author's research or expertise, as opposed to the creativity of the author's imagination.

Some of the top non-fiction books are:
 
        How Far the Light Reaches
        His Name Is George Floyd
        Atomic habits
        Mad Honey by Jodi Picoult
        Remarkably Bright Creatures
